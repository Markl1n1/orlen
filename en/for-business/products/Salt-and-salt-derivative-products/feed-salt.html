Feed salt

Safety data sheet - EN
Safety data sheet - DE

Composition

Sodium chloride – NaCl

Application

Animal food salt for fodder and feed mix manufacturing processes.

Packaging of animal feed salt

  • 50 kg open-end paper bags, placed on Euro pallets in 7 layers of 3 bags each (1050 kg),
  • 50 kg paper valve bags, placed on Euro pallets in 7 layers of 3 bags each (1050 kg),
  • 50 kg open-end plastic bags, placed on Euro pallets in 7 layers of 3 bags each (1050 kg) ,
  • 1000 kg Big Bags, placed on Euro pallets,
  • pallet sides are protected with stretch film, top with a PE hood,
  • a polyethylene or paper spacer is used between the bottom layer and the pallet.

Storage of animal feed salt

Animal feed salt should be stored in a clean, dry warehouse or store room, free from foreign smells.

Customer requirements

  • custom packaging,
  • different total product weight on the pallet,
  • other, as mutually agreed.

Distribution

Animal food salt is transported in trucks or containers. The means of transport must be clean, dry, free from foreign smells and properly tight. Salt is only released from the warehouse following a quality control andapproval of further distribution conditions.
