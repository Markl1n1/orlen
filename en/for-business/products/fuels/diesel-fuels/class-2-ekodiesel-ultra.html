Ekodiesel Ultra class 2 

Ekodiesel Ultra class 2 SAFETY DATA SHEET

Ekodiesel ULTRA class 2 is a perfect choice in winter conditions, including the Arctic or any other harsh winter climate.

Characteristics

Ekodiesel ULTRA class 2 is produced from selected hydrocarbon components derived from petroleum. Their dedicated fractional composition translates into excellent low-temperature properties of the fuel, namely cold filter plugging point (CFPP) not higher than -32 °C and cloud point not higher than -22 °C . Therefore, Ekodiesel ULTRA class 2 is a perfect choice in winter conditions, including in Arctic or any other severe winter climate.

Ekodiesel ULTRA class 2 contains a range of additives which improve its detergent-dispersant and anti-corrosive properties. Its quality is ensured through continuous monitoring across all stages of production at ORLEN's state-of-the-art advanced refinery in Płock, Poland.

Ekodiesel ULTRA class 2 contains no manganese-based metallic additives, including methylcyclopentadienyl manganese tricarbonyl (MMT).

It complies with the Minister of Economy’s Regulation of October 9th 2015 on quality requirements for liquid fuels (Dz.U. of October 23rd 2015, item 1680), the Minister of Economy’s Regulation of October 9th 2015 on quality testing methods for liquid fuels (Dz.U. of October 23rd 2015, item 1679), and the Polish standard PN-EN 590 Automotive fuels - Diesel - Requirements and test methods.

Applications

Ekodiesel ULTRA class 2 is dedicated to compression-ignition engines, in particular in wintertime when temperatures drop considerably below -20°C.
