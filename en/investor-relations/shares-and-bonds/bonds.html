Bonds

ORLEN regularly issues debt securities on both the Polish and European markets. The funds raised from the issues are allocated to the implementation of current and strategic objectives, in particular investments guaranteeing sustainable development of a modern, low-emission and resource-efficient multi-energy company.

The company currently holds in its portfolio:

  • national bond programme in the amount of PLN 4 billion;
  • foreign GMTN Programme in the amount of EUR 5 billion.

  • EMTN Programme 2021
  • GMTN Programme 2025
